About Us

Who are we?

The NGO EasyPet is an organization that offers instructions on how to create and adopt a pet. EasyPet promotes pet adoption events and collects donations to be redirected to animals that live in kennels or on the streets. The purpose of our work is: Clearing doubts about adoptions and donations. Publicize NGOs and websites that seek to help abandoned animals.Share news about Pets. Instruct our readers on how to care for their Pets.

Adoption

Why adopt a pet?

If you want to adopt a pet and contribute to our work to continue rescuing animals. Please schedule a visit to our institution, either to adopt or even to care for the animals, get in touch wherever you prefer. Most of the time, we rescue animals that have been abandoned or mistreated. Therefore, they will be much more affectionate with their owners, developing a great sense of fidelity and gratitude. Thank you in advance.
